## 📌 Project Overview
This project was completed as part of the **BCG Data for Decision Makers Job Simulation on Forage** (March 23, 2026).

The objective was to analyze a multi-channel marketing campaign and provide a **data-driven recommendation** to improve **new customer acquisition**.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem
The client wanted to understand:

> **“Which campaign + channel combination should we double down on to grow new customer sales—and why?”**

---


## 🛠️ Work Done

- Completed a job simulation focused on advising a hypothetical client on multi-channel ad campaign performance  
- Analyzed customer conversion trends using simulated data to assess campaign effectiveness across channels and audiences  
- Synthesized insights and delivered a clear, data-backed recommendation tailored to the client’s marketing goals  

---

## 📊 Analysis Approach

### 1. Data Exploration
- Reviewed campaign performance across:
  - Campaign A (Conversational)
  - Campaign B (Promotional)
- Channels:
  - Email  
  - Instagram  
  - Web Banner  

---

### 2. Customer Segmentation
- Used Excel Pivot Tables & Slicers  
- Focused specifically on **new customer sales** (key business objective)
- [Excel Analysis file](https://github.com/Tushar-Pillay/Job-Simulation-Portfolio/blob/main/BCG%20%20Introduction%20to%20Data%20for%20Decision/Campaign_Data_Excel%20Analysis.xlsx)
---

### 3. Performance Comparison
- Compared:
  - Campaign A vs Campaign B  
  - Channel effectiveness  
  - Campaign + Channel combinations  
  - [Presentation](https://github.com/Tushar-Pillay/Job-Simulation-Portfolio/blob/main/BCG%20%20Introduction%20to%20Data%20for%20Decision/Presentation.pdf)
  - [Data Visualization](https://github.com/Tushar-Pillay/Job-Simulation-Portfolio/blob/main/BCG%20%20Introduction%20to%20Data%20for%20Decision/Data_Slide_.pdf)

---

## 📈 Key Insights

- **Campaign A consistently outperformed Campaign B in new customer acquisition**
- Campaign A delivered stronger performance across all channels  
- **Email emerged as the highest-performing channel for new customer sales**
- Campaign B generated higher total sales, indicating stronger performance with existing customers  

---

## 🚀 Final Recommendation

> ✅ **Prioritize Campaign A via Email to drive new customer growth**

### Why?
- Highest new customer sales observed in **Campaign A + Email combination**  
- Strong and consistent performance across all channels  
- Best ROI for acquisition-focused marketing strategy
