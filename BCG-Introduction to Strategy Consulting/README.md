## 📌 Project Overview

This project is part of the **Boston Consulting Group (BCG) Strategy Consulting Virtual Experience Program** hosted on Forage.
It focuses on solving a business problem for a fictional luxury clothing company (ClothingCo) using structured consulting approaches.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Challenge existing mental models
* Apply structured brainstorming techniques
* Generate innovative revenue growth strategies

---

## 🧩 Key Tasks Completed

### ✅ Task 1: Challenge Mental Models

* Reframed business threats into opportunities
* Applied paradigm-shifting techniques

### ✅ Task 2: Building New Boxes

* Framed effective business questions
* Created new strategic “boxes”
* Generated actionable ideas

---

## 🧠 Approach & Methodology

* Problem framing using **“How could we…”** questions
* Challenging assumptions (old boxes)
* Building new boxes for structured ideation
* Brainstorming within defined frameworks

---

## 🚀 Final Recommendations

* Expand into new customer segments
* Launch limited-edition collections
* Strengthen digital channels
* Enhance personalized experiences
